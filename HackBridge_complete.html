How it works

Four roles. One timeline. Zero spreadsheets.

Every stage of a hackathon — from the first registration to the final leaderboard — runs through the same system, so nothing falls through the cracks between teams, judges, and organizers.

01 — Register

Teams form fast

Participants sign up, get matched into balanced teams by skill, and submit project ideas before the clock starts.

02 — Build

Track progress live

Organizers see registrations, team status, and submission counts update in real time across every track.

03 — Judge

Score with AI assist

Judges score against a shared rubric while flagged similarities and bias indices surface automatically.

04 — Announce

Publish instantly

Leaderboards, winners, and certificates go out the moment judging closes — no manual tallying.
